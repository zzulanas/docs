Splat is a REST API that takes your videos and photos and returns fully reconstructed, web-ready 3D Gaussian Splat scenes. Point the API at a video walkthrough, and within minutes you have a shareable, embeddable 3D scene — no GPU infrastructure to manage.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload your capture">
    Create a scene via the API to receive a presigned upload URL, then PUT your video, photo zip, or R3D file directly to cloud storage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start processing">
    Call the `/process` endpoint to kick off the GPU-accelerated reconstruction pipeline — structure-from-motion followed by Gaussian Splat training.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Poll for completion">
    Poll `GET /api/v1/scenes/{id}` until the status is `complete`. Processing typically takes 8–45 minutes depending on your quality preset.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download and serve">
    Download the web-optimized SOG file or raw PLY, then serve it through your app or share the Splat viewer link directly.
  </Step>
</Steps>
